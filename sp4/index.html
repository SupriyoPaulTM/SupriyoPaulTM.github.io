About Me:

Hello! I’m Supriyo Paul, a passionate Maths and Physical Science teacher from Daulatpur, South Dinajpur, West Bengal. I love helping students understand complex concepts in a simple and interesting way. Teaching isn’t just my profession — it’s something that gives me real joy and purpose every day.

In my free time, I love exploring new ideas, learning small creative projects with Python, and continuously improving my skills — whether it’s in technology or in teaching.

My goal is to grow both as a dedicated educator and a curious learner, building a life around creativity, learning, and helping others grow.
